Welcome to the Budgerigar Breeders Ring, this ring is dedicated to all the Budgerigar sites on the Internet that deal with Breeding, Showing, and Health Care of Budgerigars.

Ring Qualifications

In order for your site to join the Budgerigar Breeders ring it must follow both of these guidelines:
[1] - It must have information about Budgerigars on it.
[2] - It must have information about any or all of the following: breeding, showing, health care, or general information about Budgerigars.

PLEASE NOTE:    A site will not be let into the ring, if the ringmaster does not believe it applies to the rings purpose.
