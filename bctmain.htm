Message from the President

The Budgerigar Council is the ruling body for Tasmania and is made up from three clubs located within the State.  The clubs being:

  • Tasmanian Budgerigar Society (Est. 1933) based in Hobart
    Secretary:  Bret Dawes     - Email:  TBS@bctas.info

  • Northern Tasmanian Budgerigar Society (Est. 1935) based in Launceston.
    Secretary:   Peter Kross - Email:      NTBS@bctas.info

  • Coastal Budgerigar Society (Est. 1974) based in Devonport.
    Secretary:  Catherine Charlesworth - Email:  CBS@bctas.info

Welcome to the BCT Web Site for 2025, which has been the source of a variety of information of the budgerigar fancy for many years.  

The 3 clubs within Tasmania welcome all enquiries from members of the public wishing to join a club in their area, or maybe just seeking information. Email addresses for the clubs provided above.

As this is my first year on becoming the BCT President, I would like to congratulate our previous president Roy Blair and his team on the outstanding hosting of the ANBC National Show here in Launceston in 2024. Congratulations to all the exhibitors that progressed from either novice or intermediate to move up a status for this coming show season of 2025. There were some excellent individual results from the 2024 ANBC National Show and I wish all exhibitors the best of luck for the 2025 show season and the 2025 ANBC National Show.

The BCT and associated Societies have many shows, sales, auctions etc., around the state throughout the year, so please visit the Show Schedule Book 2025 ‘State Show Calendar’ page 6 for dates in 2025. This page also has downloads for Entry Forms ring order and show entry forms.

‘Enjoy’ the Budgerigar Council of Tasmania’s Website.
