Thank you to everybody who took part in our final survey during Issue 6 on Crested Budgerigars.
Here are the results:

CRESTED BUDGERIGARS
DESCRIPTION:

During this survey we were trying to discover the spread of the Crested variety of Budgerigars throughout the world (see pictures, below, for appearance).

SURVEY RESULTS ARE UNDER PICTURE


Above: The three varieties of crested budgerigars, from Left, Half Circular Crest,
Full Circular Crest, and Tufted Crest

THE RESULTS

We had 11 replies to this survey, and out of these there where 7 people who owned or breed crested budgerigars, and 4 who did not.

Two of the people who breed or own Crested Budgerigars were from Australia.   While other countries where the crested budgerigars can be found included USA, England, and Belgium.

This was the last Budgerigar Survey conducted by the F1 Stud Budgerigar Web Page.   The Budgerigar Survey has now been discontinued.
Thank you to all those people who took part in our surveys.
