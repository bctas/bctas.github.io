Welcome to Tasmania....

The Budgerigar Council of Tasmania invites all Budgerigar breeders/exhibitors/visitors and guests to the 2009 Australian National Championship Show which is to be staged in Burnie from 22 – 28 May 2009.

Burnie situated on the coastline of Bass Strait is Tasmania’s third largest city and located on the beautiful North West Coast. The theme for the 2009 National Show is based on the endangered Tasmanian Devil, hence the theme ‘Be a Devil in 2009 and come to Tasmania.’

Planning has gone extremely well with the many events well supported with a large number of registrations being received.   Excitement is now building with the show only two weeks out.   Late registrations will still be accepted but must now be directed by email to:  contact@national2009.com or by mail/phone to:

Judy Fletcher
PO Box 371
SORELL  TAS. 7172
Ph:  0407 874 739

For the Registrants wishing to attend evening functions their paperwork must be received by Judy no later than Sunday 17th May.    Please note all tours are now closed.   Orders are still being taken for the results booklet which will be available on the Sunday afternoon following the show.

The auction and sale information is now on this website (see the links down the right-hand side).   You will see some gaps where the details have not yet been received as Alistair will be posting this as it comes in.   The Auction birds will be on display on Saturday and the auction will be held on Saturday afternoon.   The sale birds will be on display on Sunday and the sale will be that afternoon.   For further information and details re the Auction and Sale contact Alistair Home:  auction@national2009.com

The month of May in Tasmania is cool, so come prepared for all seasons while visiting our ‘Treasure Island.’

For those not attending this year’s National, keep an eye on this site over the week prior to National for some exciting news.

Jim Fletcher
President
Budgerigar Council of Tasmania
